# HSIL Hackathon 2026 – Team Idea Workspace

A shared space for our team to collect, compare, and refine ideas for the **HSIL Hackathon 2026 – Madrid Hub**.

## Event Overview

* **Theme:** Building High-Value Health Systems: Leveraging Artificial Intelligence
* **Dates:** April 10–11
* **Location:** María Zambrano Library
* **Team Size:** 3 to 5 people
* **Format:** Two-day, in-person hackathon

The goal is to move from an initial concept to a functional prototype or operational product during the event.

## Why This Repo Exists

We’ll use this workspace to:

* Capture problem areas we care about
* Brainstorm solution ideas
* Evaluate ideas based on impact and feasibility
* Select one final concept
* Assign roles and next steps before the hackathon

## Sample Challenge Areas

* Remote patient monitoring
* Public health in urban settings and city organization
* Childhood mental health
* Hospital care and emergency management

## Questions to Answer Early

* What exact user pain point are we solving?
* What makes our solution different from existing tools?
* Why is AI necessary here?
* What can we realistically demo in the hackathon timeframe?
* How will we explain impact in a simple, convincing way?
* What are the ethical, privacy, or implementation concerns?

## Fast Brainstorming Prompts

* What hospital workflow is still too manual?
* Where do patients fall through the cracks?
* What clinical decision is repetitive and data-heavy?
* What public health issue could benefit from earlier detection?
* What could help overworked staff save time without compromising quality?
* Where is there poor coordination between professionals, services, or systems?
* What problem could be demonstrated clearly in a simple prototype?
